Skeleton in cemetery setting
Objective: survice 20min

Enemies spawn continuously around the player during the run getting more dangerous as time passes

defeated enemies leave drops to boost the player
    xp (level up lets player choose a stat boost)
    Equipment
    Artifacts
    Direct stat boost

Stats are the usual basic rpg stats
    strengh (Str) which provide weapon damage
    Agility (Agi) which provide movement speed
    Consitution (Con) which provide health
    Intelligence (Int) which provide ?
    Wisdom (Wis) which provide xp boost

Equipment can be passive effect or active weapon

player may only carry two weapons at a time

Equipment is more effective with stats (better damage, range, area of effect)

Equipment have a rarity (common, rare, legendary)
